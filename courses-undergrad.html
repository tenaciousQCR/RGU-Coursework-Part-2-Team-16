Award: BSc (Hons)

Course Length: 2 years

Mode of Study: Full-time

This course aims to equip students to work as professional engineers and consultants in the design, configuration and management of computer networks.

The course format has been designed to meet the requirements of applicants who already possess an appropriate HND, by providing a two-year top-up programme leading to an honours degree.

Entry Requirements
A relevant HND from one of our partner colleges.

Advanced Entry
Applications for advanced entry are considered on an individual basis.

For more information please visit the Robert Gordon Univeristy website.
